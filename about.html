About Us

Baking sweet memories, one graham bite at a time.

Our Story

We started this business back in high school. My friend and I were both business-minded, so we began baking and selling in Grade 10. Now that we’re in college, we’re still growing the same dream we started years ago.

Our Values

We believe in using simple ingredients and baking with love. Everything we make is fresh, handmade, and created to bring joy to everyone who tastes it.

Why Choose Us?

We make every treat fresh and handcrafted with care. From high school until now, we’ve poured our passion into improving our recipes so you can enjoy delicious baked goods made with love.
